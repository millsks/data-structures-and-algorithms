# 12-Week DSA Study Plan

## Month 1: Foundations

**Week 1: Complexity Analysis & Arrays**
- Big O notation
- Arrays and dynamic arrays
- Practice: 10 easy problems

**Week 2: Strings & Hash Tables**
- String manipulation
- Hash tables/dictionaries
- Practice: 10 easy problems

**Week 3: Linked Lists**
- Singly and doubly linked lists
- Common patterns
- Practice: 8 easy, 2 medium problems

**Week 4: Stacks & Queues**
- Implementations and applications
- Practice: 8 easy, 2 medium problems

## Month 2: Trees & Sorting

**Week 5: Binary Trees**
- Tree traversals
- Common problems
- Practice: 5 easy, 5 medium problems

**Week 6: Binary Search Trees**
- BST operations
- Practice: 3 easy, 7 medium problems

**Week 7: Sorting Algorithms**
- All major sorting algorithms
- Practice: 10 sorting problems

**Week 8: Binary Search**
- Basic and variants
- Practice: 10 medium problems

## Month 3: Advanced Topics

**Week 9: Graphs**
- DFS and BFS
- Common algorithms
- Practice: 8 medium, 2 hard problems

**Week 10: Dynamic Programming**
- DP fundamentals
- Practice: 5 medium, 5 hard problems

**Week 11: Advanced Algorithms**
- Heaps, Tries, Union-Find
- Practice: 10 medium problems

**Week 12: Review & Mock Interviews**
- Review weak areas
- Timed practice
- Practice: 15 mixed problems

## Daily Routine

1. **Theory** (30 min): Read documentation
2. **Practice** (60-90 min): Solve 1-2 problems
3. **Review** (30 min): Review solutions
